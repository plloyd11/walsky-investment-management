Privacy Policy

At Walsky Investment Management, we respect your privacy and only collect information about you that will enable us to help you and provide a greater amount of service. Any information that is collected is considered confidential. We do not share this information, nor will we offer you unsolicited materials.

Any information collected is only used to help us provide a better experience for you as the user.

Walsky does use cookies to help us monitor our website and to help us evaluate our website. Our aim is to collect only the information that we need and to remove it when we no longer need it.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please contact us at info@walsky.com
