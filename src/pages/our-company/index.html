Meet Our Team

Our Company

Walsky Investment Management, Inc. is an independent registered investment advisory firm located in Wyomissing, Pennsylvania. Consistency and stability are central to our investment style as we create personalized investment portfolios. To provide this tailored service, we have an investment team with experience and a strong knowledge of the investment industry.

We are a local firm that operates independently of large brokerage houses, allowing us to be unbiased in our advice. Since 1996, we have provided investment management services to private investors throughout the United States. Our firm offers an investment style based stocks that have growing dividends and strong fundamentals.

Our professional team has been working together for many years, which ensures our client's dependable service. Each professional is involved in making investment decisions for our clients and assisting clients with their goals.

The management team of Craig Hafer and Elizabeth Ward has more than 28 years combined experience in the investment advisory industry.
