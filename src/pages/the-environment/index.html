We care about our planet

The best investment we can make

While protecting the environment provides a greater quality of life, we realize that to have a strong and robust economy, we also need protect our environment. Our Spring, 2017 newsletter, The Environmentally-Minded Economist reviewed why protecting the environment is imperative for promoting a stronger economy. As we have long known, protecting the environment is one of the best investments we can make as a society.

Each year, Walsky Investment Management commits 5% of its pre-tax profits to not-for-profit organizations to protect the environment and to promote environmental stewardship. Over the years these organization have included, Trout Unlimited, Union of Concerned Scientists, The Green Mountain Club, and others.
